Welcome to NUCLEAR's documentation !

The NUCLEotide AssembleR is a graph-inspired solution for the in-silico fragment-based design of oligonucleotides. NUCLEAR fragments can be any standard or modified nucleotide configurations output by the MCSS docking software. The program aims to find geometrically linkable nucleotides that best interact with a receptor (usually a protein). NUCLEAR can also generate molecular hotspots by clustering the nucleotide MCSS docking results in sets of 'popular' regions of the receptor surface.


⭐ Automatic parsing of MCSS docking results
⭐ Molecular hotspots detection from nucleotide fragments
⭐ Efficient approach to the search of oligonucleotides
